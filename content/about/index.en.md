# Fueling Bold Ideas, Fast.

**123 Factory** is a cross-border startup accelerator and corporate innovation partner that connects People, Capital, and Ideas across borders to fuel growth and innovation. 

Based in **Berlin (HQ)** and **Seoul**, we bridge the vibrant startup ecosystems of Europe and Asia. Beyond simple networking, we turn vision into real momentum through practical programs and proven impact.

---

## What We Do

### 1. Startup Acceleration & Scale-Up
We provide multi-faceted support for early-stage startups with innovative ideas to find their market fit and achieve global scale-up.
*   **GtM & Market Access:** Practical strategy development and execution support for entering new markets.
*   **Funding Support:** Connecting capital and supporting fundraising for growth.

### 2. Corporate Innovation
We design collaborations with the startup ecosystem to help corporations respond to changing market environments and find new growth engines.
*   **Startup Scouting:** Identifying the best technology and teams tailored to corporate needs.
*   **Venture Governance & Investment:** Supporting venture investment strategies and governance building.

### 3. Ecosystem Building
We build communities and cultivate talent for a healthy and sustainable innovation ecosystem.
*   **Market Intelligence:** Providing the latest trends and insights from Asian and European markets.
*   **Matchmaking & Events:** Operating industrial showcases and networking for global partnership expansion.
